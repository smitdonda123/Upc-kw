Health, Safety & Environment
Health, Safety & Environment (HSE) Management is an integral and essential part of the way we conduct our business and need to be considered as part of the wider system for management of the business.

Many of the features of effective health, safety and environment management are indistinguishable from the sound management practices advocated by the proponents of quality and business excellence.

The philosophy of United Pillars is that through effective management, employment, and contracting process, we aim to be the preferred contractor and the preferred employer. Successful management of HSE issued is therefore of prime importance in all aspects of our business activities.
