Support Service
In our support services division United Pillars renders complete in support services to local companies in and outside Kuwait which deals in the following:
Accommodation: Providing accommodation facilities in apartments or villas, for any type of categories of personnel according to our client's requirements anywhere in Kuwait.
 
Catering: Providing catering services on a daily basis as per the existing international catering standards. The menu is set according to the nationalities or as per client requirements. Provision is made to arrange packed take away meals, which are delivered to required locations.
 
Laundry Service: United Pillars provides laundry service includes washing, drying and ironing of clothes which pick up and delivery as on sole entity as well as part of the support services division.
 
Transportation: With our own Fleet of busses, United Pillars organizes to and from transportation from accommodation facilities to required locations for any number of personnel, as per the client requirement.
 
Maintenance: Since this is one of our main core jobs, we have a full fledge maintenance team of about 60 Professional on our rolls to take care of electrical, plumbing, Air Conditioning and other services related to repairs and upkeep of the flats and building (security services, cleaning, pest control etc). Our support services are provided to defense personnel as well as other companies' personnel whom we accommodate from range of 250 personnel to 1500 personnel.
 
House Keeping: From our superior customer service, to your sparkling clean home. We bring all the cleaning supplies and equipment to tackle your toughest cleaning job, and leave your home or office bright and shining clean. United Pillars is a real company, staffed with local people. We are not a referral service or franchise. We send trusted, trained, background checked and e-verified employees to clean your home, never subcontractors. Because your trust, security, and piece of mind are our priority, we are licensed, insured, and for your protection.
 
Home Care : Is health care or supportive care provided in the patient's home by healthcare professionals (often referred to as home health care or formal care; in the world, it is also known as skilled care) or by family and friends (also known as caregivers, primary caregiver, or voluntary caregivers who give informal care). Often, the term home care is used to distinguish non-medical care or custodial care, which is care that is provided by persons who are not nurses, doctors, or other licensed medical personnel, whereas the term home health care refers to care that is provided by licensed personnel.
