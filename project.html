Project Services
Our objective, since inception and still enforced today, is to pursue first class accounts and negotiated projects; we are committed to making an investment in customer relations and long range marketing. Because of this philosophy, we have enjoyed repeat business and our customers have confidence in making referrals due to the faith and accountability that we have has instilled.
Procurement
United Pillars offers procurement services for domestic and overseas projects. These services can either be contracted stand alone or as part of a complete package providing greater flexibility, process improvements and lower operating costs and inventory investments.
Pre-Construction
Pre-construction service is the discovery process. Customer needs are explored and are investigated in great detail. Solutions from this early involvement result in reduced costs through coordinated trade efforts, reduced construction schedules and value engineering. We combine sourcing best practices with our expertise to develop optimal strategies prices where our competent team diving deep into your expenditure data, seeking saving potential for our clients and working on the best strategy on negotiation with the suppliers. Our work does not end with a new strategically sourced contract, but with the supplier delivering against contract specifications.
Maintenance
Planned maintenance is a proactive approach to looking after your premises. Rather than just reacting to problems as they occurred planned maintenance will keep your premises in good order, ensure you satisfy legal obligations, preserve the value of your building and minimise expensive breakdowns and repairs. Having a schedule for your planned maintenance programme will enable you to assess what maintenance work needs to be undertaken and when. You can engage a building surveyor to assist with drawing up a schedule of planned maintenance and this is often done in addition to a building survey United Pillars have been active in a wide range of maintenance projects since 1998 including execution of civil, electrical and mechanical works.
