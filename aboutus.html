Introduction
We take pleasure to introduce our selves as a United Pillars Co. General Trading & Contracting W.L.L (UPC) an experienced and enterprising company launched in 2001 by chairman Mr. Rashid Al Bous with full fledged involvement in Manpower Supply to Oil and Gas sector and the private sector as well with a paid up capital of 500,000 KWD The company has been actively involved in Manpower Supply, Support Services, and Materials Procurement Contracting Trading and Maintenance & Civil Constructions which includes large-scale civil engineering work, earth-moving, mechanical and electrical engineering, drainage and tunneling, civil engineering, dredging, survey, reclamation and pipeline engineering. We have excellent track record for successful execution of its projects works with reputed firms in Kuwait.
With over 9 years of industry experience, the company has experienced phenomenal growth and development in business volumes and net earnings and is continuously progressing with time. The estimated annual turnover is over 5 Million KD. Our company registered as a vender with Kuwait National Petroleum Company (KNPC), Kuwait Institute of Scientific Research Center and various ministries for supplying works and employees.
